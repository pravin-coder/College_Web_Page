Dr. P.SURESH BABU B.Tech., M.Tech., PhD

Professor & Head

Dr. Suresh Babu Palanisamy completed his Ph.D. in Biotechnology from Anna University, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He serves as a Professor and Head of the Department of Bioengineering at the Institute of Biotechnology, Saveetha School of Engineering, Saveetha Institute of Medical and Technical Sciences (SIMATS), Chennai, India. He completed his Masters from SASTRA University, Thanjavur & Bachelor’s degree from Anna University - BIT Campus (formerly SET, Bharathidasan University), Tiruchirappalli. Additionally, he is an Adjunct Lecturer at the Faculty of Pharmaceutical Sciences, UCSI University, Malaysia, and the Faculty of Pharmacy, University of Cyberjaya, Malaysia.

His main areas of research are Environmental Biotechnology, Marine Biotechnology, and Pharmaceutical Biotechnology. Dr. Suresh has 20 years of experience in teaching and research at various engineering colleges in India and abroad. He has published numerous scientific research articles in international peer-reviewed journals and authored multiple book chapters and review papers.

He received the Young Scientist Award from the University of Cagliari, Italy, for best presentation, and has earned various recognitions from different national and international organizations for his academic and scientific contributions. He served as an Executive Committee Member (honorary position) in the India Education Forum (IEF), organized by the Embassy of India, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, for three years starting in 2017. He facilitated 3 national level MoUs with Glaukos Algae Private Limited, Nellore, Greenmed Labs, Chennai and Neoscience Research Lab Private Limited, Chennai & 1 International MoU with University of Cyberjaya, Malaysia. Dr. Suresh is an associate member of many scientific societies and professional bodies including ISTE, BRSI, WAYS, ISRD, IAEng, etc.

Dr. R.M.SARAVANA KUMAR PhD

Associate Professor

Dr. N.SARANYA B.Tech., M.Tech., PhD

Assistant Professor (SG)

Dr. Saranya Narayanasamy is a distinguished researcher and educator, currently serving as an Assistant Professor (Senior Grade) at the Saveetha School of Engineering, SIMATS, Chennai. She received her B.Tech in Industrial Biotechnology from Anna University and M.Tech in Biotechnology from PRIST University (Gold medalist), TamilNadu, India. She received her Ph.D. from the National Institute of Technology Calicut, Kerala, India, where she conducted groundbreaking research on water pollution abatement. she has been instrumental in guiding numerous undergraduate and doctoral research projects. Her research primarily focuses on heavy metals, emerging pollutants, such as PFAS, microplastics, and pesticides, aiming to develop innovative bioremediation strategies and sustainable approaches to water pollution control.

Dr. Saranya’s impactful research has led to a substantial body of publications, including high-impact journal articles and book chapters, with a cumulative impact factor of 40 and an h-index of 11. In addition to her academic and research accomplishments, Dr. Saranya has made significant contributions as reviewer for various esteemed journals, including Science of the Total environment, Elsevier; Nature Scientific reports; Environmental pollution, Elsevier; Journal of Material Research and Technology, Elsevier; Desalination and water treatment, Taylor and Francis; Journal of Chemistry, Hindawi; International Journal of Environmental Analytical Chemistry, Taylor and Francis; Bioresource Technology Reports; Research in Ecology; Egyptian journal of petroleum Clinical and circular economy, Elsevier; Adsorption science and Technology; Energy Nexus, Elsevier. She has authored book chapters on topics such as microbial fuel cells and traditional water purification techniques.

Assistant Professor

Dr. Sambit Sarkar is an Assistant Professor and experienced researcher in biotechnology with a strong background in bioengineering.
